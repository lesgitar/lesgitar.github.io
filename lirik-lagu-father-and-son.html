20. if twice the age of son is added to age of father, the sum is 56.but if wice the age of the father is added to the age of son, the sum is 82.fund the ages of father and son.....? tolong di jawab ya​


Jawab:

son age (x) =10

father age (y) = 36

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Solve the following system:

y + 2 x = 56

2 y + x = 82

In the first equation, look to solve for y:

y + 2 x = 56

2 y + x = 82

Subtract 2 x from both sides:

y = -2 x + 56

2 y + x = 82

Substitute y = -2 x + 56 into the second equation:

y = -2 x + 56

2 (-2 x + 56) + x = 82

2 (-2 x + 56) + x = x + (-4 x + 112) = -3 x + 112:

y = -2 x + 56

(-3 x + 112) = 82

In the second equation, look to solve for x:

y = -2 x + 56

-3 x + 112 = 82

Subtract 112 from both sides:

y = -2 x + 56

-3 x = -30

Divide both sides by -3:

y = -2 x + 56

x = 10

Substitute x = 10 into the first equation:

y = 36

x = 10

30. Change into passive voiceA. My Father writes a letter to her son.B. Father and Mother read a newspaper


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : PASSIVE VOICE


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

===========================

A. My Father writes a letter to her son.

B. Father and Mother read a newspaper


=> Dilihat dari kata kerja yang digunakan di kedua kalimat di atas sudah di ketahui bahwa kedua kalimat di atas merupakan simple present tense. Jadi untuk rumus passive nya adalah :

Subject + tobe (am/are/is) + V3 + By.. Object


INGAT!!!

Subject di kalimat aktif akan berubah jadi objek di kalimat pasif.

Berikut penggunaan tobe sesuai dg subject nya :

Am => I

Are => You/they/we/nama orang plural

Is => she/he/it/nama orang singular.


Jadi kalimat passif nya adalah :

1). A letter is written to her son by my father.

2). A newspapet is read by father and mother.
