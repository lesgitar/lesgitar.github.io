19. Q5!+4!=?Nt: Lagu Enak Berisik✓ (Break your neck) VS Lagu Enak GK Berisik✓(Know you better)​


※ 5! + 4!

= (5.4.3.2.1) + (4.3.2.1)

= (20.3.2.1) + (12.2.1)

= (60.2.1) + (24.1)

= (120.1) + 24

= 120 + 24

= 144 ☑

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

5! + 4! = (5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1) + (4 × 3 × 2 × 1)

= 120 + 24

= 144.
