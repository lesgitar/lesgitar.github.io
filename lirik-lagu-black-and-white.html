23. A game consists of black and white pieces. The number of black pieces is5 more than 3 times the white pieces. 7 white and 15 black pieces areremoved each round. After several rounds, there are 3 white and 56 blackpieces left. How many pieces were there in the beginning?​


Jawab:

If:

B = Total amount of black pieces

W = Total amount of white pieces

B = 3W + 5

Each round: W - 7, B - 15

After several rounds there are:

W = 3

B = 56

The calculation will be if we compare W with B:

Last round = 3 : 56

1st previous round = 10 : 71

2nd previous round = 17 : 86

3rd previous round = 24 : 101

4th previous round = 31 : 116

5th previous round = 38 : 131

6th previous round = 45 : 146

7th previous round = 52 : 161

Because on the 7th previous round B = 3W + 5 then the white and black pieces in the beginning is: 52 white pieces and 161 black pieces.
