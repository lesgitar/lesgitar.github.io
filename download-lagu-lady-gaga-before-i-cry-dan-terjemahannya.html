# SEMOGA MEMBANTU.

Jawaban dari pertanyaan tersebut adalah C. I conclude that the lady knew the man.

Subject : English

Category : Modals

Sub-category : Modal perfect

Level : SHS

Code : 5

The lady must have known the man before. This means I conclude that the lady knew the man. (C)

must + have + past participle digunakan untuk menyatakan dugaan positif tentang suatu peristiwa di masa lampau yang didapat dari pengamatan logis.

Contoh lain  

He must have forgot to fill in the gas yesterday - the car tank is almost empty.

Dia pasti lupa mengisi bensin kemarin - tanki mobilnya hampir kosong.
