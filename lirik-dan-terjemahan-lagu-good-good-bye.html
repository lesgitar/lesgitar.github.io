20. When do we say: •good morning •good bye •good night

- good morning is used when greeting someone in the morning

- good bye is used when parting with someone

+ good night greeting used when meeting someone at night or saying goodbye to someone at night

good bye digunakan ketika kita mengucapkan salam perpisahan setelah bertemu dengan seseorang
