8. If you want to check your CD how should you hold the CD


Jawaban:

When handling a CD (Compact Disc), it's important to hold it properly to avoid any damage. Here are some tips:

1. **Hold by the Edges**: Always hold a CD by its outer edge. Never touch the shiny, reflective surface with your fingers as it can leave smudges and fingerprints that can interfere with the disc's readability.

2. **Use the Center Hole**: You can also put your finger through the center hole of the CD for a more secure grip.

3. **Avoid Scratches**: Be careful not to scratch the disc. Even small scratches can cause skips or prevent the CD from playing entirely.

4. **Keep it Clean**: If you need to clean the CD, wipe it gently with a soft, lint-free cloth from the center of the disc straight out to the edge. Do not wipe in a circular motion as this could cause more damage.

5. **Storage**: When you're not using the CD, store it in its case to protect it from dust and scratches.

Remember, improper handling can cause permanent damage to a CD, affecting its longevity and performance.

Jawaban  Pendahuluan  

Asking for permission adalah ungkapan yang digunakan saat kita ingin meminta izin kepada orang lain. Dalam menggunakan expression asking for permission, kita perlu memperhatikan apakah situasinya formal atau tidak formal, apakah orang yang kita ajak bicara lebih tua atau tidak.

Berikut adalah beberapa ungkapan yang bisa digunakan dalam asking for permission, yaitu:

Would you mind If......? (Apakah anda keberatan jika saya....?)

Would it be allright if....? (Apakah tidak apa-apa jika saya....?)

Do you mind if.....? (Anda keberatan apabila saya....?)

Is it okay if......? (Anda tidak apa-apa jika saya....?)

 Can I......? (Dapatkah saya....?)

May I.....? (Bolehkah saya....?)

NB: Semakin ke atas, semakin sopan ungkapannya.
